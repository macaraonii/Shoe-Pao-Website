Shipping
Delivery providers, timelines, fees, and tracking.
Delivery Providers

Sellers arrange delivery with logistics providers. Shoe Pao facilitates communication but delivery is performed by the chosen logistics provider.

Timelines

Estimated delivery times are shared in listings and at checkout. Processing typically occurs within 1–2 business days before handoff to the courier. Cross‑border deliveries may be subject to import/export rules and longer timelines.

Fees

Shipping fees are displayed at checkout and may vary by destination, service level, and parcel weight/size. Surcharges may apply to remote areas. Cash‑on‑Delivery availability depends on the selected courier.

Tracking

Tracking details are provided once the order is dispatched. For updates, contact the courier using the provided tracking number or reach us via the contact options on our About page.
